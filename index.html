We specialize in delivering pharmaceutical APIs, nutritional supplements, food additives, cosmetic raw materials, agricultural chemicals, and veterinary chemicals to clients worldwide.

Our Mission

Since our establishment, our unwavering commitment has been to act as a reliable partner for businesses—from startups to global enterprises—by streamlining their supply chains, enhancing product quality, and driving sustainable growth. Whether you’re formulating a new pharmaceutical drug, developing a breakthrough cosmetic, or advancing agricultural solutions, Shengyuan Chemical is dedicated to your success.

Our Products

  • Pharmaceutical APIs: Compliance-driven solutions meeting international standards (USP, EP, JP) for global pharmaceutical manufacturers.
  • Nutritional Supplements: Premium ingredients for dietary supplements, vitamins, and functional foods, formulated for safety and efficacy.

  • Cosmetic Raw Materials: Cutting-edge actives, emollients, and botanical extracts for skincare, haircare, and personal care.
  • Agricultural Chemicals: Reliable inputs for crop protection, soil enrichment, and sustainable farming to support global food security.
  • Veterinary Chemicals: High-purity ingredients for animal pharmaceuticals, nutritional supplements, and feed additives.
